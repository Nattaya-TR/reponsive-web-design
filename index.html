Daily motivation

When you change the quality of your thinking,

you change the quality of your life,

sometimes instantly.

Just as positive words can make someone smile

or a well-timed humorous quote can make someone laugh,

our thoughts react to the world in real-time.

Gratitude

In positive psychology research,
